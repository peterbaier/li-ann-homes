About

The heart behind Li-Ann Homes

Our company was created to support landlords in building a life of freedom, choice and security – with the confidence that their investment is safe, respected and working for them.

We focus on creating a genuine win–win partnership. You can sit back and enjoy life, knowing your property is cared for, your income is reliable, and your time is your own.
